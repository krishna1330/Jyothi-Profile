About Me

I’m Jyothi, a passionate and dedicated beautician with a love for helping people look and feel their best. With 2 years of experience in the beauty industry, I specialize in a range of services, including makeup, hair styling, and personalized beauty treatments.

Whether you’re preparing for a special occasion or simply treating yourself to some self-care, I am here to bring your beauty vision to life.
